Refunds & Returns

Here's my refund policy for purchased assets:

When I'll Issue Refunds

  • Asset Doesn't Work: If the asset is legitimately broken and I can't fix it, you'll get a refund.
  • Missing Files: If essential files are missing from the package and I can't provide them.
  • Incompatibility Issues: If the asset doesn't work with the advertised Unity/VRChat SDK versions and can't be made to work.

When I Won't Issue Refunds

  • Buyer's Remorse: Changed your mind? Sorry, digital products can't be returned once downloaded.
  • User Error: If the asset works but you're having trouble implementing it (though I'm happy to help troubleshoot!).
  • After Extended Use: If you've been using the asset for weeks/months and then ask for a refund.
  • Project Changes: You decided to change your world concept and don't need it anymore.

If you're having issues, hit me up on Discord first. Most problems can be solved with some troubleshooting, and I genuinely want to help you get things working.
